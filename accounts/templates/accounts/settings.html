Role Permissions
Overview of what each role can access
👑 Captain

Full system access — user management, all modules, system settings, backup & export, and reports.

📋 Secretary

Access to residents, facilities, institutions, and reports. Can add, edit, and delete records. Cannot manage users or backup.

🩺 Health Worker

Access to residents module and health-related reports only. Can view and update health and disability information.

💰 Treasurer

Access to reports and socio-economic data. Read-only access to resident records.
